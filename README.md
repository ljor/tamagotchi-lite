# tamagotchi-lite

Welcome to Tamagotchi-Lite

The goal of the game is to keep your pet alive until it dies of old age (at 35)
Keep its hunger, sleepiness, and boredom levels below 10. If any of those reach 10, your pet wil die.

Have fun!
